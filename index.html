About Josué Dubon

I’m Josué Dubon, a professional soccer player with experience in El Salvador’s first division. As a pro athlete, I developed key skills in teamwork, discipline, and tactics. Now, I’m passionate about sharing this knowledge to help young players take their game to the next level.

One-on-One Session

Each training session lasts one hour and focuses on enhancing the key aspects of your game. We will work on your dribbling, passing, shooting, and overall game understanding. These personalized sessions are designed to help elevate your soccer skills, build your confidence on the field, and take your game to the next level. The price for each session is $40.
