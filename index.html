About Josué Dubon

I’m Josué Dubon, a former professional soccer player from El Salvador. After several years playing in the first division as a winger, I transitioned into coaching. Now, I dedicate myself to helping young athletes improve their skills and take their game to the next level. My training programs are personalized to focus on individual growth and improvement.

Training Services

Private Sessions

One-on-one coaching focused on dribbling, passing, shooting, and overall game understanding. Each session is 60 minutes, designed to maximize improvement and build confidence.

Speed & Agility Training

Focused on improving your athleticism with specialized drills designed to increase speed, agility, and endurance on the field.
