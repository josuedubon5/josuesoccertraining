About Josué Dubon

I’m Josué Dubon, a professional soccer player who spent several years playing in El Salvador's top division. As a winger, I developed key skills that allowed me to compete at the highest levels. I’m passionate about helping young athletes unlock their potential by sharing the knowledge and skills I gained during my career. My goal is to guide players towards mastering essential techniques like dribbling, passing, and shooting while also enhancing their game understanding.

Private Soccer Training

One-on-One Session

Each session is one hour, focusing on improving key aspects of your game. We will work on your dribbling, passing, shooting, and overall game understanding. These personalized sessions are designed to help you take your soccer skills to the next level and build confidence on the field.
