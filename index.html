About Me

Hi, I'm Josué Dubon! I have had the privilege of playing professional soccer in El Salvador for several years, where I honed my skills on the field and developed a deep understanding of the game. Throughout my career, I played as a winger, contributing to my team’s success both offensively and defensively. My time in El Salvador’s first division taught me the importance of discipline, teamwork, and continuous improvement.

Now, as a coach, I’m dedicated to helping players of all levels improve their skills and reach their full potential. Whether you're looking to improve your dribbling, passing, shooting, or overall soccer IQ, I’m here to help you succeed!

Services Offered

Private Sessions

Each session is one hour long, where we will work on developing your child's key soccer skills, including dribbling, passing, and shooting. These sessions are personalized to help each player improve their technique and overall understanding of the game in a fun, supportive environment.

Speed & Agility

Focused drills to increase your child's speed, agility, and overall fitness for soccer. These sessions are designed to help young players become quicker on the field and improve their reaction times during match situations.
